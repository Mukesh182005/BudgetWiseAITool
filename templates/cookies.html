Cookie Policy

How BudgetWise uses cookies and similar technologies

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

BudgetWise uses cookies for the following purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Authentication, security, and session management. Required for the app to function. Session
Functional Remembering your preferences like dashboard layout and currency settings. 1 year
Analytics Understanding how users interact with our platform to improve features. 90 days
Performance Monitoring page load times and app performance for optimization. 30 days

3. Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for BudgetWise to operate. They include:

  • session: Maintains your login session securely
  • csrf_token: Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks
  • remember_token: Enables "Remember Me" functionality

You cannot opt out of essential cookies as they are required for the service to function.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on BudgetWise.

5. Third-Party Cookies

BudgetWise does not use any third-party advertising or tracking cookies. We do not share cookie data with advertisers.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Check this page periodically for updates.

7. Contact

For questions about our use of cookies, contact us at privacy@budgetwise.app.

🍪 Cookie Consent

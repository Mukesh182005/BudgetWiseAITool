Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

1. Information We Collect

BudgetWise collects the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Financial Data: Income details, expense records, budget allocations, and savings goals that you voluntarily enter
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, including features used and time spent

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information and screen resolution
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Pages visited and actions taken within the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing personalized budgeting, forecasting, and financial health insights
  • Powering AI-driven features such as expense categorization and spending predictions
  • Improving our platform through analytics and user behavior patterns
  • Sending important account notifications and optional financial tips
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing unauthorized access

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use bcrypt for password hashing and JWT tokens for secure session management. All financial data is stored locally in an encrypted database and is never shared with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

BudgetWise does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research or analytical purposes only.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your financial data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal and financial data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

BudgetWise is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification. Continued use of BudgetWise after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@budgetwise.app.

🍪 Cookie Consent

We use cookies to enhance your experience, manage sessions, and analyze our traffic. By clicking "Accept All", you consent to our use of cookies according to our Cookie Policy.
